Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about my web development services.

How long does it take to complete a website?
The timeline varies depending on the project complexity. A simple website typically takes 2-3 weeks, while more complex projects may take 4-8 weeks. I'll provide you with a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.
Do you provide website maintenance services?
Yes, I offer ongoing maintenance packages to keep your website secure, updated, and running smoothly. This includes regular backups, security updates, content updates, and technical support.
What technologies do you work with?
I'm proficient in a wide range of modern web technologies including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (React, Vue, Angular), PHP, WordPress, WooCommerce, and various database solutions. I can recommend the best technology stack based on your specific requirements.
Will my website be mobile-friendly?
Absolutely! All websites I develop are fully responsive and optimized for all devices including desktops, tablets, and mobile phones. Mobile-friendly design is not an option but a standard practice in my development process.
What do I need to provide to get started?
To get started, I'll need some basic information about your business, your goals for the website, any brand guidelines you have, and content (text and images). Don't worry if you don't have everything ready – I can guide you through the process and help with content creation if needed.
